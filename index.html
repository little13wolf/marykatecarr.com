Drupal

This is a simple Client Managment System designed and implemented in Drupal. Initially, this was designed to only track donors and their donations but was extended through multiple iterations. The system now can track donor and volunteer information, export CSVs to be used in mail-merge mailing lists and generate a variety of reports based on different criteria, including a year-end cumulative donation report.
